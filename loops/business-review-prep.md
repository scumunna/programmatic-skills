# Business review prep

Assemble a monthly or quarterly business review from the period's results, reviewed for accuracy before it goes to the client, stopping when the review is clean.

- **Use when:** Ahead of a monthly or quarterly business review.
- **Action:** Pull the period's performance by objective and the path to conversion (`reporting-by-campaign-goal`, `path-to-conversion-analysis`, the platform measurement skills) with the `reporting-analyst` agent. Draft the narrative, wins, misses, learnings, and the plan for next period with the `client-communications-lead` agent. Then have the `qa-scrutinizer` agent review it.
- **Verify:** The scrutinizer confirms the numbers are sourced, the attribution claims hold, the wins are not overstated, the misses are stated honestly, and each recommendation is supported by the evidence. Independent reviewer, not the author.
- **Stop:** Ready for the client team once the review is clean. Loop once to fix raised issues, then re-review. Blocked if the period's data is incomplete.
- **Guardrails:** Do not send or present the review. Delivery requires human approval. No number without a source. State uncertainty plainly. No em dashes in the client text.
- **Handoff:** The client team finalizes and presents the approved review.

Prompt:
> Before the business review, assemble the period's results by objective plus the path to conversion, and draft the narrative (wins, misses, learnings, next-period plan). Then have it independently reviewed for sourced numbers, valid attribution, honest framing, and supported recommendations. Fix what the review raises and re-review. Do not send or present it. Stop at a clean draft for the client team.
